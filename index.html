About Me

Passionate builder for 3 years

I'm Polo, a self-taught Roblox builder and modeler. What started as a hobby became a real mastery of 3D world design in Roblox Studio.

My current project — a multi-biome game — features forest, desert, Japan, frozen tundra and a neon urban zone. Each biome is designed with its own assets, atmosphere and unique gameplay.

I craft every model by hand, optimize every scene and refine every detail to make each world immersive and memorable.
